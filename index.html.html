The River of Dharma

A braided chronology of Buddhist vehicles, schools and lineages. Each stream is a living tradition: it springs from an older current, widens with influence, narrows with decline — and some run dry. The breadth of every channel suggests its relative weight in its own age.

● text  ·  ◆ collection  ·  ◎ author
Stream widths are indicative of relative historical prevalence, not exact measurements — the sources themselves flow and disagree, and the dates of many texts are scholarly approximations. Original names are given in Pāli, Sanskrit, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Burmese and Tibetan. Texts are placed on the stream in which they arose; collections stand for whole families of scripture.
